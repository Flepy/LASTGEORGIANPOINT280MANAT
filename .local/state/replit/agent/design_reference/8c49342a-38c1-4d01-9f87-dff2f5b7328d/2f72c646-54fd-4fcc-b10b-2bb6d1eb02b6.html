About Georgian Point

Bringing authentic Georgian flavors to Baku with sophisticated dining and warm hospitality.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for authentic Georgian cuisine, we bring the rich culinary traditions of Georgia to the vibrant city of Baku. Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our Mission

To provide an unforgettable dining experience that captures the essence of Georgian hospitality, featuring the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods in an elegant atmosphere.
